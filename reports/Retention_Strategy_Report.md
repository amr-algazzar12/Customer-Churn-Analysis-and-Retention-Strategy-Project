## 2. High-Risk Customers (>70% Churn Probability)

### 2.1 Profile

**Demographics:**
- 45% are senior citizens
- 65% have no partner or dependents
- Average tenure: 6 months

**Services:**
- 80% on month-to-month contracts

| **20-30% Discount Offer** | Sales | 20-30% ACV | 45% retention |
| **Premium Support Upgrade** | Support | $500/yr | 20% retention |
| **Contract Lock-in Offer** | Sales | Varies | 35% retention |

#### Recommended Package

**"VIP Retention Program"**

âœ… **Immediate Benefits:**
- 25% discount on next 12 months
- Free premium tech support (24/7)
- Free device protection
- Dedicated account manager
- Priority customer service

âœ… **Requirements:**
- Commit to 1-year contract minimum
- Switch to automatic payment
- Participate in quarterly review

**Investment:** ~$2,500 per customer (25% of $10,000 ACV)

**ROI:** $5,000 saved - $2,500 spent = $2,500 net / customer
